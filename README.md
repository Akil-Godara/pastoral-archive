
# Pastoral Archive

A digital archive documenting pastoral nomadic life, traditional herding practices, and indigenous knowledge systems across mountain and grassland regions.

**Live Website:** [pastoral-archive.pages.dev](https://pastoral-archive.pages.dev/)

---

## 📖 About

The Pastoral Archive is a comprehensive digital repository that preserves and shares the rich cultural heritage of pastoral communities. This project documents traditional ecological knowledge, migration patterns, livestock management practices, and the evolving relationship between herders and their environments.

### Mission
To create an accessible, researcher-friendly archive that:
- Preserves indigenous pastoral knowledge
- Supports academic research on nomadic cultures
- Provides resources for descendants of pastoral communities
- Documents climate change impacts on traditional herding practices

---

## 🌍 What's Inside

### Core Collections
- **Oral Histories** - Interviews with elder herders and community leaders
- **Migration Maps** - Historical and contemporary pastoral routes
- **Photographic Archives** - Visual documentation of pastoral life
- **Research Papers** - Academic studies on pastoralism and ecology
- **Traditional Knowledge** - Indigenous practices in livestock management
- **Climate Impact Studies** - Documentation of environmental changes

### Geographic Focus
- Mountain pastoral regions
- High-altitude grasslands
- Seasonal migration corridors
- Traditional grazing territories
